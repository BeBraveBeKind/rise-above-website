About Rise Above

I'm Michael Browne, founder and principal of Rise Above partners. Just like you, I live and breathe my business. After nearly two decades in the marketing trenches—working with the outdoor and tech brands you know — I started Rise Above to help small and midsize businesses meet their moment.

In a world where the latest tools are a click away, leaders still need seasoned expertise to shape strategy, tell their story, and use resources wisely. That’s where I come in.

Think of me as your on-demand marketing brain: part strategist, part implementer, part outsourcer, part sorcerer. Someone who knows the shortcuts, avoids the pitfalls, and delivers results (not just decks).

Great marketing isn't louder. It's sharper, smarter, and grounded in thoughtful strategy.
